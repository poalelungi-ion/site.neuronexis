How secure is NeuroNexis?

All sensitive data is stored locally on your PC or device with end-to-end encryption and biometric access.

Which plans run locally?

Pro, Business, Enterprise, and Corporate plans run as secure apps on compatible PCs and devices.

What devices are supported?

Pro and Business plans support home-compatible PCs and select mobile devices (check compatibility list).

How does computer vision work?

Processes images locally for object detection and visual automation, keeping data on your device.

Can it integrate with other tools?
